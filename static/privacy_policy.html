2. Description of the data collected

We are only collecting what we need from our users to protect ourselves legally in case of misuse of the resources put online on hack.courses and mandatory data required by the services provided.

  • IP Addresses and requests made to our HTTP servers are collected in order to be able to track potential malicious use of the platform.
  • Emails are stored for authentication.
  • Usernames are stored for identification.
  • Subscription dates and last login dates are stored when creating an account and logging into the platform.
  • Passwords are hashed using a non-deprecated hashing algorithm as of today, hashes are stored in our database for the login to function.
  • Metadata regarding the user's services are stored (such as quizzed finished and scores, workstations deployed and terminal histories as non exhaustive examples).
None of your data is shared with any third party.

Data can be stored for up to 1 year. You can opt-out at any time by deleting your account on https://auth.hack.courses.

You can contact us at https://fenrir.pro/contact if you have any question regarding your data.
